Why Metal Edge

A trade. An ownership stake. A career worth having.

ESOP Ownership

Every employee earns shares in the company through our ESOP program. The better Metal Edge performs, the more your retirement account grows — because you own the results.

Union Wages & Benefits

Local 11 Sheet Metal Workers wages, full health coverage, pension, and apprenticeship training. We follow union standards because our people deserve them.

Skilled Craft Work

You'll work on real Midwest commercial projects — hospitals, corporate campuses, retail facades — using techniques that take years to master. Work you can point to from the street.

Safety First

OSHA partnership, Local 11 safety training, and our annual Safetyfest mean every crew member goes home safe. Safety isn't a checkbox here — it's the standard.

Midwest-Based

Headquartered in Romeoville, IL. Most projects within 150 miles — you sleep in your own bed most nights. No long-haul travel unless you want it.

Room to Grow

Apprentices become journeymen. Journeymen become foremen. Foremen become project managers. We promote from the field because that's where the real expertise lives.

Current openings

Sheet Metal Journeyman

Field Local 11 Union Full-Time
Apply

Install architectural sheet metal systems on commercial projects across the Midwest — ACM panels, coping, fascia, standing seam, column covers, and specialty metals. You'll work from engineered shop drawings and hold yourself to tight tolerances.

  • Journeyman card through Local 11 or reciprocal SMWIA local
  • 5+ years field experience with architectural sheet metal
  • OSHA 10 or 30 preferred
  • Able to work at heights up to 200 ft
  • Valid driver's license

Sheet Metal Apprentice

Field Union Training Program Full-Time
Apply

Learn the trade from the ground up alongside experienced journeymen. You'll rotate through shop fabrication and field installation, earning your hours toward journeyman status while getting paid to learn a skill set that lasts a lifetime.

  • Enrolled in or eligible for Local 11 apprenticeship program
  • Mechanical aptitude and willingness to learn
  • Reliable transportation
  • Must be 18+ and authorized to work in the US

Shop Fabricator

Shop Romeoville, IL Full-Time
Apply

Work in our in-house fabrication shop producing custom architectural sheet metal components — coping, fascia, column covers, flashings, and specialty pieces — from engineered shop drawings. 95% of what we install is made in-house, and precision in the shop is what makes field installation clean.

  • 3+ years sheet metal fabrication experience
  • Proficiency with brakes, shears, and roll forming equipment
  • Ability to read shop drawings and engineering specs
  • Experience with Kynar and anodized aluminum finishes preferred

Estimator

Office Romeoville, IL Full-Time
Apply

Prepare competitive bids for architectural sheet metal scopes — takeoff from architectural drawings, quote materials, price labor, and coordinate with GCs and owners from bid through award. You'll work across ACM panels, coping systems, wall systems, and specialty metals.

  • 3+ years estimating experience in sheet metal, curtain wall, or commercial envelope
  • Ability to read architectural and structural drawings
  • Experience with On-Center, Planswift, or equivalent takeoff software
  • Strong communication skills with GCs and owners

Project Manager

Office + Field Romeoville, IL Full-Time
Apply

Manage architectural sheet metal projects from award through closeout — submittal coordination, shop drawing review, schedule management, RFI/change order processing, and field oversight. You'll be the primary point of contact for GCs and owners on active projects.

  • 5+ years project management in commercial construction
  • Familiarity with architectural metal systems (ACM, IMP, coping, fascia)
  • Experience with Procore or similar construction management software
  • Strong understanding of the submittal and RFI process
  • Ability to read and interpret architectural and structural drawings
